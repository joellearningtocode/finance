4. Model Assumptions & Calculation Logic

A summary of the rules and formulas powering your forward projections.

Pre-Retirement Phase

  • Baseline: Starts from your latest live account balances.
  • Growth: Monthly compounding using your selected Real Growth Rate.
  • Pre-Retirement Taper: Growth drops to 30% speed in the 2 years prior to retirement to model portfolio de-risking.
  • One-Offs: Planned pre-retirement withdrawals are deducted from ISAs in April.

Post-Retirement Phase

  • Day 1 Mortgage Payoff: Clears full outstanding mortgage balance using ISAs.
  • 50/50 De-risked Growth: Portfolio grows at 75% of pre-retirement rate (blending growth and safe assets).
  • ISA Bridge (Pre-58): Living expenses draw tax-free from ISAs until pensions unlock.
  • Pension Drawdown (58+): Living expenses switch to pensions with a 20% basic tax rate applied.
  • Timeline Horizon: Projects cash flows until Emma reaches age 90.
